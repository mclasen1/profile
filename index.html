Maja Clasen

New York City journalist focused on health & science and audio-based stories.

Reach me at majaclasen@gmail.com

I began reporting while studying at Binghamton University, where I covered campus events for the student-run newspaper, Pipe Dream. Since then, I've contributed writing to the NYCity News Service and Canarsie Courier.

I am working towards my master's degree in journalism from the CUNY-Craig Newmark Graduate School of Journalism here in NYC, specializing in the medium of audio. Most recently, Iinterned at the healthcare trade publication MedPage Today.

Interests

Although a majority of my work focuses on topics like healthcare, medical research and local community concerns, I love fashion and try to see free exhibits when I can.

I love getting involved with my own community as well. Having recently moved to Queens, I had to check out the food and artwork being sold by artists and residents alike at a recent street fair.

Working in New York City, I've covered everything from City Council meetings, peaceful demonstrations and rowdy protests. I aim to include thorough, easy to understand, and accurate coverage of social issues in my work.
